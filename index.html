Sunnydale Ocean Week

Schedule

Whale-watching cruise departing from the Sunnydale Marina. Tickets are $35 for adults and $25 for children.

Ocean Week dinner at The Lighthouse Restaurant featuring guest speaker Dr Lillian Bell, Director of the Sunnydale Marine Institute. Tickets are $55 per person.

Oceans Alive. Free movie screening every hour at the Sunnydale Cinema.

Global Warming Conference at the Sunnydale Convention Centre. Registration fee of $60 includes lunch and admission to all sessions.

Free guided tours of the Sunnydale Aquarium every hour.

Open water exploration of Shark Island. Cruise departs from Sunnydale Marina. Tickets are $35 for adults and $25 for children.

Captain Jack's Pirate Show. Free children's show three times daily at Sunnydale Park.
